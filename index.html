Who We Serve
 
 
 
We want to be every student’s go-to resource for all things science. While some websites strictly offer step-by-step math articles, some publications provide up-to-date news content and others feature fun science project ideas, Sciencing does it all. We’re a dynamic site with content spreading across three distinct categories — Homework Help, Projects/Inspiration, and News/Editorial. Through relevant editorial content that shows how science affects your day-to-day life and visually awesome science experiments, our goal is to make Science relevant and fun for everyone. Whether you need help solving quadratic equations, inspiration for the upcoming science fair or the latest update on a major storm, Sciencing is here to help.
    Sciencing strives to serve students who are:
  • Pro-active: The Sciencing reader actively seeks answers to scientific questions.
  • Curious: The Sciencing reader likes learning new things – regardless if it’s about a type of rare spider or how the latest hurricane is linked to climate change.
  • Looking for specific information: The Sciencing reader is looking for specific scientific information, ranging from a science project idea to answers to their math or physics questions.
Sciencing is your trusted math textbook, your favorite professor and your news-junkie classmate, all in one. We’re full of facts, here to patiently walk you through the toughest problems, and we’re armed with science news headlines to keep you up-to-date. Above everything else, though, we’re here to help you learn, and help make science fun.
